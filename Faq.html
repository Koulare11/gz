FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is GZ and how is it used?

G-Z is a completely free web application that allows users to enter their birth date, birth country, and gender without prior registration. It is designed to generate global and national statistics on births, including:

  • Birth counts by day, month, and year.
  • Distribution by country, gender, and age.

The main goal is to provide accessible demographic data for a better understanding of global population trends.

2. Are my data anonymous?

Yes, your data is completely anonymous. We designed G-Z to ensure that:

  • No data can be traced back to a single user.
  • We do not collect information that identifies who submitted what. Rest assured, your identity is protected and your data is aggregated immediately.

3. How will I know if my data has been submitted?

Once your data has been submitted:

  • The submit button will change color: orange during submission and green when successfully submitted.
  • The button text will change from "Submit" to "Submitted".

These indicators confirm that your data has been taken into account.

4. Can I modify or delete my submissions?

Yes, you have the option to delete your data immediately after submission. Here’s how:

  • A reset icon will appear after submission.
  • By clicking this icon, your data will be completely removed from our servers.

However, once you close the web application or the session expires, the data becomes irrevocably anonymized and cannot be modified or deleted. Please refer to our Privacy Policy '5' for more information.

5. What happens if I provide incorrect information?

We encourage you to provide accurate and verified information to ensure the quality of the statistics. If you notice an error after submission:

  • Use the reset icon to delete your data.
  • Then you can submit the corrected information.

6. Why should I support GZ?

We aim to develop G-Z into a mobile application where user registration will be recommended. This will allow us to provide even richer statistical data, including:

  • A study on the challenges of life, both internal and external.
  • Information on causes of death in specific locations.
  • Many other useful statistics!

By participating in our surveys, you will help gather data that can assist in identifying solutions to current social challenges. Please note that part of the contributions may also be used to reward the developer's efforts.

7. Why should I follow GZ on social media?

By following us on social media, you can:

  • Stay informed about new analyses and demographic trends.
  • Discover regular updates about our global statistics.
  • Participate in educational discussions on global demographic issues.

Additionally, we share interesting information based on our statistics and keep you informed about our new projects.
